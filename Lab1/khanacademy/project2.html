Recipe #1: Nasi Lemak

  • Time: 60 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 2 cups
Coconut milk 2 cups
Water 1 cups
Salt 1 tsp
Dried anchovies 0,5 cups
Boiled eggs 2
Cucumber slices 1 cup
Sambal (chili paste) 0,5 cups

Step 1: Wash the rice thoroughly and drain.

Step 2: In a rice cooker or pot, combine rice, coconut milk, water, pandan leaf, and salt. Cook until rice is fluffy.

Step 3: Fry the dried anchovies until crispy and set aside.

Step 4: Boil eggs, peel, and cut them in half.

Step 5: Arrange rice on a plate and top with sambal, anchovies, boiled eggs, and cucumber slices.

Step 6: Serve warm and enjoy!

Recipe #2: Char Kway Teow

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Flat rice noodles 250g
Prawns 150g
Bean sprouts 1 cup
Chinese chives 0,5 cup
Soy sauce 2 tbsp
Dark soy sauce 1 tbsp
Dark soy sauce 3 cloves
Eggs 2
Cooking oil 3 tbsp

Step 1: Heat oil in a wok over high heat.

Step 2: Add minced garlic and stir-fry until fragrant.

Step 3: Add prawns and cook until pink.

Step 4: Push the prawns aside and crack eggs into the wok. Scramble lightly.

Step 5: Add noodles, soy sauce, and dark soy sauce, and toss well.

Step 6: Add bean sprouts and chives, and stir-fry for another 2 minutes.

Step 7: Serve hot with lime wedges if desired.

Source: Rasa Malaysia - Char Kway Teow

Last Hero

The world grew quiet, the sky turned gray
A lone soul walked where shadows lay.
No crown to bear, no songs to sing,
A weary heart, a broken wing.

Through fire and frost, through endless night,
They held the line, the fading light.
Not for glory, nor for gain,
But to shield the world from pain.
When dawn awoke, the hero gone,
Their sacrifice lived on and on.
